ABOUT ME

We love yoga

My name is PAPPU YADAV. I was born in the holy abode of LORD SHIVA, on the banks of the river GANGA/GANGES. I have always had a deep attachment to spirituality and have been fortunate to come into contact with many spiritual teachers throughout my life. Finally, SRI RAM JI MALVIYA, the holy Professor of Yoga Tantra and former Vice Chancellor of Sampurnananada Sanskrit University, Varanasi, became my GURU. He gave me the name SIDDHARTHA PAPPU and has taught me Yoga Tantra Practices, Hatha Yoga Therapy, and Yogi Patanjali's Yoga Sutras.

Famous for Hatha Yoga and Pranayama, Siddharth Yoga classes are headed by Yogi Siddharth. He is a former student of Sri Ram Ji Malviya, the holy professor of Yoga Tantra and has a graduate degree in Yoga Tantra along with the knowledge of Yogi Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras. Visit these classes for an enriching experience at Mansarovar Ghat.
